sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: Overall, it took the longest time but was close to Sort2 when the list was already sorted.

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: Overall, it is by far the fastest one at sorting and is increasingly faster in compared to the other sorts as the amount of data being sorted increases.

sort3 uses: Selection Sort

How do you know?: Overall, it is the second fastest except when the list is already sorted in which case it took far longer. Even if a list is sorted, selection sort still has to loop through all the numbers checking for the lowest value for each position.
