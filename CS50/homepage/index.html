Introduction

My name is Grey and I am currently 33-years-old. I've been a bit of a PC nerd since I was young but only recently became almost one-pointedly interested in coding.

That isn't to say that I never utilized it in the past. Afterall, I grew up when the internet and computers were beginning to become more widely available.

My earliest experiences coding was using HTML to personalize my AIM away message and my Myspace profile page.

The first home computer that we had was a family Christmas present in the mid 1990s.

I remember wishing I could sneek onto the internet without my parents knowing, but it was impossible with how loud it was to connecting to the internet.

I remember how much joy I would experince when the internet connection was finally picked up because it didn't always work.

If you're curious about what it was like, turn down your sound and check out this video of signing into AOL via Dial Up in 1998.

For a bit more more check out CBS Evening News: 1996 AOL Goes Down for 19 Hours .
