PRECALCULUS

 

Required text

Course Description

Precalculus is an intermediate level mathematics course on the basics of algebra and trigonometry. Course topics include functions, systems of equations, basic trigonometry, matrix algebra, basics of conic sections, sequences and series, basic probability, and the introduction to limits.

Course Objectives

         Define, identify and evaluate functions, their domains and ranges, and their graphs.

         Solve systems of 2-, 3- and higher-variable linear equations.

         Simplify trigonometric functions by manipulating trigonometric identities.

         Perform basic matrix algebra operations.

         Identify which type of conic section is associated with a given equation.

         Find the sums of finite arithmetic and geometric series.

         Apply basic probability rules to problems involving uncertainty.

         Define what limits are and how they are used.
