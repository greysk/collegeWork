Course Description

This course is a functional approach to algebra that incorporates the use of appropriate technology. Emphasis will be placed on real-life applications of the fundamental concepts of algebra with an emphasis on equations, functions and their graphs, and applying mathematical models.

Course Objectives

·         Apply the fundamental concepts of algebra, including complex numbers and logarithms

·         Solve a wide variety of equations and inequalities

·         Construct equations of common functions based on given properties

·         Graph common functions and equations

·         Identify the properties of a variety of functions by their graphs and equations

·         Show proficiency with functions and their operations

·         Solve systems of equations

Credit Hours: 3

Prerequisites: None
